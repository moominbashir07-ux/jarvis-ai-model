# JARVIS AI OS - User Manual

Welcome to **JARVIS AI OS**, a production-quality AI desktop assistant designed to behave like a real desktop AI operating system.

---

## 🎙️ Wake Up & Voice Interaction

1. Say **"Hey Jarvis"** aloud.
2. The HUD interface will animate into a listening circle state, expressing:
   > *"Yes Momin, how can I help you?"*
3. Speak naturally. You can describe research requests, computer automation instructions, or ask general questions.
4. Voice responses will play through your speaker while transcription text appears on the HUD.

---

## 🛠️ Automated Commands Examples

### 1. Research Requests
Ask JARVIS to find online topics and verify timelines:
* *"Research the latest AI computer chips and summarize them."*
* *"Google nuclear fusion net energy gain perspective."*

### 2. Desktop Control
Describe actions to manipulate your workspace:
* *"Open Chrome and search for weather."*
* *"Create a folder named project_alpha and write a summary file."*
* *"Read my clipboard."*

### 3. Vision & Element Clicks
Ask JARVIS to look at your display:
* *"Find the Save button and click it."*
* *"Locate Cancel on the screen."*

---

## 🔒 Security & Approvals
For safety, destructive operations (such as permanently deleting files outside the Recycle Bin or modifying system workspace settings) may require explicit authorization:
* JARVIS will pause and prompt you: *"Awaiting User Approval..."*
* Confirm or reject the task on the HUD.
* You can adjust automatic permissions inside the `.env` settings.
